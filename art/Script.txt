INTRODUCTION:

SCENE 1: At the top of Upstate New York... At the heart of Rochester...

SCENE 2: Lies a realm of learning, harboring talents destined to shape the future of our world.

SCENE 3: Scientists, Engineers, Entrepreneurs... Athletes, actors, artists. Doctors, musicians, leaders, managers, elites of the world. These aspirants of betterment unite under these campuses every day, striving towards greatness.

SCENE 4: Greatness that harbors potential and impact. Greatness that can save lives... Greatness that can foster kindness and peace.

SCENE 5: Guided by their astute professors and faculty, they challenge adversity towards their futures...

SCENE 6: And powering their resolves, adorning their beloved Rush Rheese, is their symbolic Dandellion, the Golden Floret, shining brightly with the strength that allows them to call Meliora.

SCENE 7: At least... it used to...

SCENE 8: One day, the flower lost its shine, and was soaked in dark.

SCENE 9: With its yellow hue drained, it stopped supplying students and faculty with drive, and not long after...

SCENE 10: Inhabitants became unmotivated. 

SCENE 11: Grades fell, projects failed, friendships were faltered, and the future, once bright with potential, became bleak. Indeed, the river became shrouded in metaphorical and literal darkness.

SCENE 12: Even so, not everyone faltered... As from the edge of dark rose a yellowjacket... The Yellowjacket.

SCENE 13: The one beloved by many, carrying the spirits of all. Rocky, the University's admirable mascot, could not watch his friends and teachers fall to a mere illness, for he knew their potentials were at stake.
